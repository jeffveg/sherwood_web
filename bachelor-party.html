Bachelor & Bachelorette Parties

Skip the ordinary — go out with a legendary adventure

ArcheryTag® is one of the most fun and competitive group activities available in the Phoenix area — and it is unlike anything your crew has done before. Two teams, bows, foam-tipped arrows, and a field. The result is fast, hilarious, and completely memorable.

We come to you. Pick your venue — backyard, park, church parking lot, or any open space — and we bring everything. Setup, equipment, staffing, and teardown all handled.

Why It Works for a Bachelor or Bachelorette Party

  • Everyone plays — no athletic experience required; the foam-tipped arrows level the playing field completely
  • Competitive and exciting — team vs. team format naturally creates unforgettable moments and bragging rights
  • Safe and pain-free — virtually no impact from the foam tips; safe enough for all fitness levels
  • Easy to organize — we run every game; the host just shows up and enjoys the day
  • Works anywhere — outdoors in a park or backyard, or indoors with enough space

How a Typical Event Runs

  • We arrive and set up the field at your chosen location
  • Our staff gives everyone a safety briefing and quick archery lesson
  • Teams are divided and games begin — multiple rounds of competition
  • We manage all the game play from start to finish
  • We pack up and leave when you are done

Activity Options

  • ArcheryTag® — Starting at $600
    The full team competition experience. Ages 10 and up. Normal field 62 × 30 ft or Small field 62 × 15 ft (3 per side). Works indoors or outdoors.
  • Combo Package — Starting at $800
    Add HoverBall® target shooting alongside ArcheryTag® for extra variety.
