Youth Group Activities

Activities teens actually get excited about — we run the games, you lead your group

Finding activities that genuinely excite teenagers is hard. ArcheryTag® does it every time. Sherwood Adventure brings the full setup to your location — equipment, safety instruction, and game management — so you can stay focused on your students while we handle the fun.

You lead. We run the games. Our staff handles all instruction, officiating, and gameplay from start to finish. Youth leaders can participate, supervise, or just watch — your call.

Why Youth Leaders Choose Us

  • Teens love it — fast-paced, competitive, and different from anything they have done before
  • Safe enough to feel comfortable — foam-tipped arrows, required facemasks, and a proven safety record since 2011
  • No experience needed — our staff teaches everyone in minutes; no archery background required
  • Natural community builder — team gameplay creates real moments of connection without feeling forced
  • Works anywhere you meet — church parking lot, gym, outdoor field, park, or retreat center

Great For

  • Youth group nights and weekly programs
  • Weekend retreats and overnight events
  • Lock-ins and late-night activities
  • End-of-year celebrations and kickoffs
  • Outreach events for teens in the community
  • Boys and girls ministry events

Activity Options

  • ArcheryTag® — Starting at $600
    The main event. Team vs. team competition with bows and foam-tipped arrows. Ages 10 and up. Normal field 62 × 30 ft or Small field 62 × 15 ft (3 per side).
  • HoverBall® — Starting at $150
    Target shooting activity, great for mixed ages or when space is limited. Ages 7 and up.
  • Combo Package — Starting at $800
    Run both for larger youth events or when you want variety throughout the night.

Tournaments for Youth Groups
