What to Expect

When you book with Sherwood Adventure, here is what happens:

  1. You tell us your event date, location, and approximate group size.
  2. We confirm availability and send you pricing and details.
  3. We arrive at your location, set up all equipment, and brief your group on safety rules.
  4. Our staff runs the games the entire time — you relax and enjoy your event.
  5. When the fun is done, we pack up and leave the space the way we found it.

Which Activity Is Right for My Group?

  • ArcheryTag — Best for ages 10 and up. Normal field 62 × 30 ft, or Small field 62 × 15 ft (up to 3 players per side). Great for church youth groups, corporate team building, and teen birthday parties.
  • HoverBall — Great for ages 7 and up. Fits in a 20 × 15 ft space with a 9 ft minimum ceiling. Perfect for festivals, indoor church events, school activities, and smaller gatherings.
  • Combo Package — Want both? We can run ArcheryTag and HoverBall at the same event for larger groups or when you want something for every age.

Questions Before You Book?

We are happy to answer any questions about space requirements, pricing, age recommendations, or anything else. Reach us by phone or through our contact form and we will get back to you quickly.
