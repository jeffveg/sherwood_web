Church & Faith Group Events

Safe, wholesome fun the whole congregation can enjoy — we come to you

Churches across the Phoenix Metro and Southwest Valley trust Sherwood Adventure to bring excitement to their events — from family picnics and VBS to youth nights and church-wide celebrations. We deliver clean, safe, competitive fun that works for every age group in your congregation.

No venue needed. We set up at your church parking lot, fellowship hall, outdoor space, or any venue you choose. We bring everything and handle all setup, staffing, and teardown.

Why Churches Love Us

  • Family-friendly and alcohol-free — clean fun for all ages with no concerns about content or safety
  • Something for everyone — HoverBall® for younger children (ages 7+), ArcheryTag® for teens and adults (ages 10+)
  • We run the games — our staff handles all instruction, officiating, and game management so your team can focus on your people
  • Works at your location — church parking lot, gymnasium, fellowship hall, outdoor pavilion, or community park
  • Fully insured — liability documentation available upon request

Popular Church Event Formats

  • Family picnics and church-wide celebrations
  • Vacation Bible School (VBS) activity stations
  • Youth group nights and lock-ins
  • Men's and women's ministry events
  • Outreach events open to the community
  • End-of-year celebrations and appreciation events

Which Activity Fits Your Event?

  • HoverBall® — Starting at $150
    Great for mixed-age groups, younger children, and smaller spaces. Ages 7 and up. Needs just 20 × 15 ft indoors or outdoors.
  • ArcheryTag® — Starting at $600
    Fast-paced team competition for teens and adults. Ages 10 and up. Normal field 62 × 30 ft or Small field 62 × 15 ft.
  • Combo Package — Starting at $800
    Run both activities side by side — something exciting for every age group at your event.

Ready to Plan Your Event?

Tell us your event date, expected attendance, and available space and we will put together a plan that fits your church perfectly.
