Legendary Fun, Delivered to You

Archery-based games for every age and every event size

Archery Tag

Think dodgeball with bows and foam-tipped arrows. Two teams face off in a fast-paced, safe, and completely pain-free game — a perfect icebreaker for coworkers, an exciting activity for youth groups, and a crowd favorite at any group event.

We bring all the equipment, set up the field, and referee every game. All you do is show up ready to play.

Ages 10 and up  |  Space: ~50 × 80 ft (basketball court size)  |  Indoors or outdoors

HoverBall

Our inflatable archery target is a fantastic option for festivals, church picnics, school events, and smaller gatherings across the Phoenix area. Participants of all ages take aim at floating ball targets for a fun, low-pressure archery experience.

Sets up in a compact space — perfect for indoor or outdoor events where room is limited. No archery experience needed.

Ages 7 and up  |  Space: 20 × 15 ft, 9 ft ceiling min  |  Indoors or outdoors

Combo Package

Want both? Our Combo Package brings Archery Tag and HoverBall together for larger events — giving your guests variety and keeping everyone engaged throughout the day.

Perfect for festivals, company events, and church family days where you want something for every age group.

Great for larger groups  |  Ask us about pricing and space requirements

We Come to Your Location

No venue needed — we bring the adventure to you

Sherwood Adventure was built on a simple idea — everyone deserves legendary fun. Our name comes from the legend of Robin Hood and Sherwood Forest, a place where the everyday person was the hero. We bring that spirit to every event we serve in the Phoenix Metro area.

We are a fully mobile archery tag company, meaning we come to your location — whether that is a church parking lot, a company campus, a community park, or a festival grounds. Our staff handles all setup, safety instruction, and game management so your event runs smoothly from start to finish.

We use lightweight bows and foam-tipped arrows that are safe and pain-free, and we teach proper archery safety before every game. Serving church groups, youth ministries, corporate teams, and event planners across the Phoenix Metro and Southwest Valley since day one.
