Host a Tournament

Bring competitive archery tag to your church, league, or community — we run the whole thing

Ready to take archery tag to the next level? Sherwood Adventure can host a full elimination tournament at your location in the Phoenix Metro area. We handle the brackets, the equipment, the field setup, the officiating, and the digital scorekeeping — you just bring the teams.

Tournament management is included. Our online tournament system at app.sherwoodadventure.com handles team registration, brackets, and live results so players and spectators can follow along.

What We Provide

  • Full Archery Tag equipment for all participants
  • Field setup and breakdown at your location
  • Safety instruction and rules briefing before play
  • On-site staff to referee all matches
  • Elimination bracket management via our tournament app
  • Digital scorekeeping and results — viewable by everyone

Space Requirements

Each Archery Tag field requires approximately a basketball court-sized area — about 50 × 80 feet. Tournaments work indoors or outdoors. We can advise on field layout based on your specific venue.
