Host a Tournament

Bring competitive ArcheryTag to your church, league, or community — we run the whole thing

Ready to take ArcheryTag to the next level? Sherwood Adventure can host a full elimination tournament at your location in the Phoenix Metro area. We handle the brackets, the equipment, the field setup, the officiating, and the digital scorekeeping — you just bring the teams.

ArcheryTag® is a registered trademark of Archery Tag® LLC. Learn more at archerytag.com

Tournament management is included. Our online tournament system at signup.sherwoodadventure.com handles team registration, brackets, and live results so players and spectators can follow along.

What We Provide

  • Full ArcheryTag equipment for all participants
  • Field setup and breakdown at your location
  • Safety instruction and rules briefing before play
  • On-site staff to referee all matches
  • Elimination bracket management via our tournament app
  • Digital scorekeeping and results — viewable by everyone

Space Requirements

ArcheryTag fields come in two sizes — Normal (62 × 30 ft) and Small (62 × 15 ft, up to 3 players per side). Tournaments work indoors or outdoors. We can advise on field layout based on your specific venue.
