Corporate Team Building

Get your team out of their heads and into action — we come to your location

The best team building doesn’t feel like team building. ArcheryTag® puts your team in a fast-paced, competitive environment where titles don’t matter — communication, trust, and strategy do. Sherwood Adventure delivers the full experience to your company campus, parking lot, or any outdoor space in the Phoenix Metro area.

Fully managed, zero prep required. We handle all logistics — equipment, setup, safety instruction, game management, and teardown. Your event organizer just picks the date and location.

Why It Works for Corporate Groups

  • Levels the playing field — no athletic advantage; the bows require technique, not strength, so everyone competes equally
  • Natural icebreaker — shared adrenaline breaks down professional barriers faster than any workshop
  • Encourages real communication — team gameplay requires coordination, strategy, and trust under pressure
  • No experience needed — our staff teaches everyone; participants are competing within minutes
  • Comes to your campus — parking lot, courtyard, warehouse floor, or outdoor grounds
  • Fully insured — liability documentation available upon request for your HR or facilities team

Popular Corporate Event Formats

  • Company picnics and outdoor team days
  • Department team-building sessions
  • Quarterly meetings and all-hands celebrations
  • New employee onboarding and orientation activities
  • Client appreciation and partner events
  • Holiday parties with a competitive twist
